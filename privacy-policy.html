Privacy Policy — Social Media Assistant

Last updated: July 19, 2026

This app ("Social Media Assistant") is an internal tool built and used by Rakiza Company LTD (operating as Ampplify Academy) to manage our own Facebook Page and Instagram Business Account. It is not distributed to, or used by, any external client, customer, or third party.

What data we access

Through the Facebook/Meta Graph API, the app accesses only data belonging to our own Facebook Page and linked Instagram Business Account, specifically:

  • Our Page's identity (name, ID) and connected Instagram Business Account identity (username, ID)
  • Post content we create ourselves (captions, images, videos) — used to publish to our own Page/Instagram account
  • Engagement numbers (likes, comments, shares) on our own past posts — used only to help our own team plan future content

We do not access, collect, or store data belonging to any other Facebook Page, Instagram account, or individual user.

How we store and use this data

  • All data (drafts, captions, post history, engagement numbers) is stored locally in a SQLite database on our own company computer — it is never uploaded to any cloud server we don't control.
  • AI caption/content generation runs on a local AI model (Ollama) on the same computer — post content is not sent to any third-party AI service.
  • Data is used solely to operate the app's own features: drafting content, publishing to our Page/Instagram, and reviewing our own post history.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any Platform Data with any third party. Data leaves our computer only to reach Meta's own servers (to publish posts and read our own Page's data), using our own access token.

Data retention and deletion

Data is retained locally for as long as we choose to keep it for our own record-keeping. Since this is an internal, locally-run tool with no external user accounts, any team member with access to the app can delete drafts, media files, or the local database directly at any time. To request deletion of any specific data, contact us using the details below.
